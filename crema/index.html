The Ritual

Three steps, every time

  1. 01

    Weigh

    Eighteen grams, ground fine and dosed level. Consistency starts on the scale.

  2. 02

    Pull

    Twenty-seven seconds at nine bars. We watch the crema turn from blonde to tiger.

  3. 03

    Pour

    Milk steamed to sixty degrees, folded glossy, and poured slow into the cup.
